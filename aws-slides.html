MARKET CONTEXT

Why are we here? The Legal Mandate.

It is not just a trend; it is government policy. In 2017, the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) issued Department Circular 002.

"Cloud First Policy"

"Gov't agencies shall prefer cloud computing solutions... to reduce costs and increase efficiency."

This triggered a massive migration in:

  • GovTech: SSS, Pag-IBIG digital portals.
  • FinTech: GCash, Maya (scaling on AWS).
  • Enterprise: Jollibee Group, PAL.

The Singapore Region (ap-southeast-1)

Where 90% of PH workloads live. It has 3 Availability Zones (Data Centers). It is massive, secure, and resilient.

ACCOUNT SAFETY

Don't get Bankrupted

Cloud is "Pay as you Go." If you leave the tap running, you pay for the water.

The Horror Story

"A student in Quezon City uploaded his AWS Access Keys to a public GitHub repo by mistake. Bots found it in 5 minutes. They spun up 50 servers for crypto mining. He woke up to a $15,000 bill."

The Protection

  • 1 MFA on Root: Non-negotiable.
  • 2 Budgets: Set a budget of $1.00. Get emailed if you hit $0.80.
  • 3 Git-Secrets: Use tools to scan code before pushing to GitHub.
